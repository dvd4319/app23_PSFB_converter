Figure 6.2 shows a photograph of the converter prototype, consisting of two 1.6 kW phase-shift full-bridge modules connected in parallel. For easier visualization, the converter has been divided into the following sections:

  • Input voltage terminals (Vin);
  • Primary section of Module 1 and Module 2;
  • T1, T2, the power transformers;
  • Control section, including the microcontroller along with driver and protection modules;
  • Controlled rectifiers of Module 1 and Module 2;
  • L1, L2, the output inductors;
  • Converter output voltage terminals (Us).
