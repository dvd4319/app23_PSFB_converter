9 Converter Loss Analysis

9.1. Output Inductor Losses

Power losses in the output inductors: $$ P_{L_o} = P_{DCR,L_o} + P_{core,L_o} = {{ results.P_Lo}} ~ W $$ Power dissipated due to the DC winding resistance of the inductor: $$ P_{DCR, L_o} = I_{L_o, rms}^2 \cdot R_{L_o} = {{ results.P_DCR_Lo}} ~ W $$ Modified Steinmetz Equation: $$ P_{MSE} = C_m \cdot F_{eq}^x \hat{B}^y = {{ results.P_MSE_mW_cm3}} \frac{m W}{ cm^3 } $$ AC component of the flux density in the magnetic core: $$ \Delta B_{L_o, ac} = \frac{V_{in} \cdot \frac{N_s}{N_p} - V_o}{N_{L_o} A_c} \cdot \frac{D}{F_{sw}} = {{ results.delta_B_ac_T}} ~ T $$ Equivalent frequency for arbitrary (non-sinusoidal) current waveforms (rectangular, PWM): $$ f_{eq} = \frac{2}{\pi^2} \cdot F_{sw} \cdot \frac{1}{D \cdot (1-D)} = {{ results.f_eq_kHz}} ~ kHz $$ Specific losses per volume: $$ P_{MSE} = C_m \cdot f_{eq}^2 \frac{\Delta B_{L_o,ac}^y}{2} \cdot \frac{mW}{cm^3} = {{ results.P_MSE_mW_cm3}} ~ \frac{m W}{ cm^3 } $$ Power loss in the magnetic core: $$ P_{core,L_o} = P_{MSE} \cdot V_c = {{ results.P_core_Lo}} ~ W $$

9.2. Primary-Side Power Switch Losses

Primary-side power static switches: $$ P_{PR} = P_{cond, PR} + P_{sw,PR} = {{ results.P_PR}} ~ W $$ Conduction losses on one primary-side power static switch: $$ P_{cond, PR} = I_{PR,RMS}^2 \cdot R_{dson,PR} = {{ results.P_cond_PR}} ~ W $$ Switching losses: $$ P_{sw,PR} = \frac{1}{8} \cdot \left( 2 \cdot C_{oss} + C_{tr} \right) \cdot V_{in}^2 \cdot F_{sw} = {{ results.P_sw_PR}} ~ W $$

9.3. Secondary-Side Power Switch Losses

Conduction losses: $$ P_{cond, SR} = I_{SR, RMS}^2 \cdot R_{dson, SR} = {{ results.P_cond_SR}} $$ Switching power loss: $$ P_{sw,SR} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot C_{oss,SR} \cdot \left( V_{in} \cdot \frac{N_s}{N_p} \right) \cdot F_{sw} = {{ results.P_sw_SR}} ~ W $$ Total losses for one rectifier branch: $$ P_{SR} = P_{cond, SR} + 2 \cdot P_{sw,SR} = {{ results.P_SR}} ~ W $$

9.4. Power Transformer Losses

Power dissipated in the primary winding resistance $R_{prim}$: $$ P_{t_{res,prim}} = I_{p_{RMS}}^2 \cdot R_{prim} = {{ results.Pt_res_prim}} ~ W $$ Losses due to the secondary winding resistance $R_{sec}$: $$ P_{t_{res,sec}} = I_{L_o, rms}^2 \cdot R_{sec} = {{ results.Pt_res_sec}} ~ W $$ Peak flux density in the magnetic core: $$ \hat{B} = \frac{V_{in} \cdot D}{2 \cdot N_p \cdot A_e \cdot F_{sw}} = {{ results.B_hat_T}} ~ T $$ Specific losses (per volume): $$ P_{MSE} = C_m \cdot f_{eq}^{x} \cdot \Delta \hat{B}_{L_o,ac}^y \cdot \frac{mW}{cm^3} = {{ results.P_MSE_tr_mW}} ~ \frac{mW}{cm^3} $$ Power losses in the transformer magnetic core: $$ P_{t_{core}} = P_{MSE} \cdot V_c = {{ results.Pt_core}} ~ W $$ Total transformer losses: $$ P_t = P_{t_{res,prim}} + P_{t_{res,sec}} + P_{t_{core}} = {{ results.Pt}} ~ W $$

9.5. Control Circuit Power Consumption

$$ P_{DRV} = V_{cc} \left( 4 \cdot Q_{g_{PR}} + 4 \cdot Q_{g_{SR}} \right) \cdot F_{sw} = {{ results.P_DRV}} ~ W $$

9.6. Losses Due to Voltage Stress Limiting Circuits

$$ P_{snubber} = 2 \cdot C_s \cdot \left( V_{in} \cdot \frac{N_s}{N_p} \right)^2 \cdot F_{sw} = {{ results.P_snubber}} ~ W $$
