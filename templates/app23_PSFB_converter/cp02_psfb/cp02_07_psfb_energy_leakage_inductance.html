7. Available Energy Calculation in Leakage Inductance

Energy stored in the parasitic capacitances on the primary side: $$ E_{cap} = \frac{1}{2} \left( 2 \cdot C_{oss} + C_{tr} \right) \cdot V_{in, \max}^2 = {{ results.E_cap_uJ}} \mu C $$
  1. $C_{oss}$ - drain-source parasitic capacitance
  2. $C_{tr}$ - estimated parasitic capacitance of the transformer
  3. $V_{in, \max}$ - maximum input voltage of the converter
Energy stored in the leakage inductance: $$ E_k = \frac{1}{2} \cdot L_k \cdot I_{p_{\min},2}^2 = {{ results.E_k_uJ}} \mu C $$
